Market Insights & Investment Thesis

AI Infrastructure is Consolidating

The next 18 months will see major consolidation in AI infrastructure. Winners will have defensible data moats and clear path to profitability.

Investment Opportunity: Look for AI companies with proprietary datasets and usage-based revenue models showing unit economics improvement.

Vertical SaaS is Still Early

Industry-specific software remains massively underserved. Opportunities in healthcare, logistics, and professional services.

Investment Opportunity: Target vertical SaaS with 10+ year industry expertise and built-in distribution through industry relationships.

Fintech is Maturing

The infrastructure layer is built. Next wave is specialized solutions for underserved niches with clear regulatory paths.

Investment Opportunity: Focus on embedded fintech and B2B2C models with demonstrated compliance expertise.
