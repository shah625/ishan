My Favorite Markets Right Now

Accounting/Finance AI Tools

Having worked in finance, I see how much manual work still exists. AI can automate reconciliations, expense categorization, and financial reporting that currently takes hours.

Why I love it: Clear ROI for customers, sticky workflows once implemented, and massive TAM across every business that handles money.

Aerospace Market

Space is becoming accessible to startups, not just governments. From satellite manufacturing to launch services, there's real innovation happening at much lower costs.

Why I love it: High barriers to entry create defensible businesses, government contracts provide stable revenue, and the market is expanding rapidly.

Personal Health Market

People are taking more control of their health data and decisions. There's opportunity in tools that help individuals track, understand, and act on their health metrics.

Why I love it: Direct-pay models avoid insurance complexity, high customer engagement, and growing consumer willingness to pay for health optimization.
